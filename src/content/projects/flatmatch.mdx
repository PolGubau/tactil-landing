We created a solution that makes the accommodation search process **fast, visual, and engaging**, leveraging interaction patterns that users already know and love.

Gamifying the apartment search with a Tinder-like experience **accelerates decision-making and increases engagement**. It has had a great impact on Erasmus students and young professionals.

**Why does this improve the experience?**

- Reduces search time by 70%
- Eliminates decision fatigue
- Makes the process more fun and less stressful


One of the biggest challenges was making posting accommodation **quick and frictionless**. We designed a guided 9-step flow that turns a complex process into something simple and accessible for any user, even without prior experience.

**Impact:**
- Users save an average of 8-12 properties before making a decision
- 65% of contacts are made from the favorites section

**By the Numbers**
- **500+ users** in just 3 months without marketing investment
- **45% retention** - Users come back because it works
- **25 swipes per session** - 2.4x higher engagement than competitors
- **70% less time** searching for accommodation (from 2-3 hours to 15-20 minutes)
- **8 out of 10** find viable options in their first session
- **3x more interactions** than traditional platforms
