## Acetate - Custom Vinyl Manufacturing for DJs and Musicians

**Acetate** is a B2C ecommerce platform that empowers DJs and musicians to order custom vinyl records in small batches or large volumes. It features complete payment orchestration with Stripe, admin order management, and fulfillment tracking.
